**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Kind:** patch | new-skill
**Target:** path/to/SKILL.md
**Status:** pending | accepted | rejected | applied

One line.

## Evidence (k≥3)

- …

## skill-quality gate

- [ ] invocation/description
- [ ] completion criteria
- [ ] disclosure

- [ ] positive steering
- [ ] not project-memory-in-skill
